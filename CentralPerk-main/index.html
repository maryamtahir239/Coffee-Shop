about us

what makes our coffee special?

All of our coffee beans are directly sourced from our partner coffee farmers and cooperatives, and we believe it is our responsibility to pay them a fair and reasonable price for their product. We know that by doing this, we create mutual, lasting partnerships with growers whose coffee beans elevate our product. All the farmers and cooperatives we work with are vetted to make sure the beans are farmed at the highest ethical standard.

We understand the importance of attention to detail in every step of the coffee production process. From sourcing, roasting, blending, packaging, and shipping, we utilize proven processes and quality controls at every step to ensure the quality of our beans.
